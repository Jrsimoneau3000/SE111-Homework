Bolt (2008)

Bolt is a dog who has lived his whole life on the set of a TV action show, believing his superpowers are real. When he is accidentally shipped to New York City, he must make his way back across the country to his owner Penny with the help of a street cat named Mittens and a hamster named Rhino.

Company Credits: Walt Disney Pictures, Walt Disney Animation Studios

Release Date: November 21, 2008

Genres: Animation, Adventure, Comedy, Family

Rating: PG

Running Time: 96 min

Finding Nemo (2003)

When his son Nemo is taken by a scuba diver and placed in a fish tank in Sydney, Australia, an overprotective clownfish named Marlin sets out across the ocean to find him. Along the way he is joined by a forgetful but lovable fish named Dory.

Company Credits: Pixar Animation Studios, Walt Disney Pictures

Release Date: May 30, 2003

Genres: Animation, Adventure, Comedy, Family

Rating: G

Running Time: 100 min
